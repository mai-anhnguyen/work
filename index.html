Introduction

Hello! My name is Mai-Anh, and I am a student at Brown University double concentrating in Computer Science-Economics and Modern Culture and Media - the equivalent of a film major at Brown! I am passionate about creating in innovative ways, whether it be through innovative technology or short films. In terms of hobbies, I was formerly a competitive contemporary jazz dancer on a pre-professional company, so I still enjoy dancing in my free time. My favorite styles are modern, ballet, and contemporary. I'm dancing at my best when it's to a good song, so I also enjoy listening to new music. I love trying new restaurants and logging my experiences on my Beli.
